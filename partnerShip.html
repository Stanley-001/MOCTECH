A Corporate Sponsorship

Powerhouse in Nigeria

StanPay has earned the reputation of being a prominent name and powerhouse in corporate sponsorship in Nigeria, based on its objectives of sponsoring and partnering on initiatives which expand its brand visibility and reach in the areas which matter, whilst also being innovative, creative and dynamic thus bringing distinctive and unique dimensions to the organisation.

The Bank's underlying sponsorship philosophy is aligned with its strategy, the ability to meet business objectives, opportunities for networking and hospitality, resource priority and brand awareness.

The variety and scale of StanPay's sponsorship doesn't only ensure that there is something to appeal to all tastes and preferences but also that the gold standard is experienced across the length and breadth of Africa. The most prominent categories that StanPay sponsors are: Golf, Polo, Lifestyle events in the creative sector such as Fashion, Arts and Music amongst other various strategic sponsorships.

Each initiative is chosen based on its compliance with the bank's sponsorship policy and guidelines.
